üí° Pro Tips

Write down your script word-for-word and practice 2-3 times
Record multiple takes and pick the best one
Smile while recording (it comes through in your voice!)
Use simple language (avoid too much technical jargon)
Show results, not code (instructors want to see it working)
Emphasize database integration ("data is fetched from Oracle", "SQL query executes", etc.)
Highlight business rules (security deposit, operating hours, etc.)
